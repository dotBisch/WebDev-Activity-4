Our Korean Culinary Journey

Traditional flavors, modern presentation, heartfelt hospitality

From Seoul to Your Table

Our story begins with a passion for authentic Korean cuisine

2010

Roots in Seoul

Chef Park Min-jun opens his first restaurant in Gangnam, Seoul, specializing in traditional Korean BBQ with a modern twist.

2015

Crossing the Pacific

Inspired to share Korean culinary traditions abroad, Chef Park relocates to the United States and begins developing the Seoul Bites concept.

2018

First Location Opens

The inaugural Seoul Bites restaurant opens in Koreatown, quickly becoming a local favorite for both traditional and modern Korean dishes.

2023

Expanding Our Reach

Seoul Bites earns recognition as one of the top Korean restaurants in the city, featured in multiple culinary publications.
